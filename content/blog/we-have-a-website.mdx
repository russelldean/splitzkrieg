(forget splitzkrieg.org that's old news)

If you didn't happen to notice the tiny little lettering on the back of your scoresheets yesterday announcing the rollout of this site,
you are not alone.  Let me repeat most of it here - 

The first thing to do is look yourself up at the top, and check out what's there.  Then maybe do some exploring - your team has a page, playoffs are in here,
milestones, a ticker....

There is a message/feedback icon that follows you around - if you see any errors or have suggestions or other feedback, use it liberally.
This is still under construction....

And you ask - Why oh why did I swallow this fly?  

Years ago, I said I wanted to make us a site like baseballreference.com, where all the stats could live and everybody
would get to have their very own dedicated page of their history, and I wasn't playing.

*I like data and it's been lovely to find out a bunch of you all like data, and we've generated a lot of data over the years, so what else were we gonna do with it all?

*The spreadsheets have done a heroic job for us all these years, but the bi-weekly process of keeping everything in spreadsheets is a chore,
and it was time to automate further.

*Watching people try to find old bowling stats by checking through their emails was starting to get sad.

*I wanted to learn how to do some things I hadn't.  Yes, I have extra time on my hands.  No, I don't need an intervention.
